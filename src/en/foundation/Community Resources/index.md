## Community & Funding Requests

The Ceph Foundation is committed to supporting our community through various resources and funding opportunities. We are here to help if you have a project idea, need support for a community initiative, or want to propose improvements to the Ceph ecosystem.

## Ceph Foundation Community Request Form

The Community Request Form is your channel for submitting requests, suggestions, and proposals to the Ceph Community Manager and Governing Board. We welcome ideas that help strengthen and grow the Ceph community.

### What can you request?

- Technical development or feature requests
- Documentation improvements
- Community programs and events
- Infrastructure and services
- Marketing and outreach initiatives
- Education and training programs
- Governance and policy suggestions
- Other community-driven initiatives

### How it works

The Ceph Foundation Governing Board reviews all submissions and will work on approved items that align with the Foundation's mission and available resources.

Please provide as much detail as possible to help us understand and evaluate your request effectively. You will receive a response regarding the status of your submission.

[**Submit a Community Request**](https://form.asana.com/?k=f6mdd2cDTRFLO-bgiN_xWw&d=9283783873717)

## Ceph Foundation Funding Request

The Ceph Foundation provides funding support for projects and initiatives that advance the Ceph ecosystem and benefit the broader community. If you're working on something that aligns with our mission, we encourage you to apply.

### Funding opportunities may include:

- Development of new features or capabilities
- Research projects related to distributed storage
- Community events and conferences
- Educational content and training materials
- Infrastructure for testing and development
- Open source tools that benefit the Ceph ecosystem

### How to apply

Please complete the funding request form with as many details as possible to help us understand the complete scope projects.

The Governing Board reviews all funding requests, considering factors such as community impact, alignment with Foundation goals, available resources, and project feasibility.

[**Submit a Funding Request**](https://form.asana.com/?k=7aCHVRhp0x1Ga1nOCXlckQ&d=9283783873717)

## Questions?

If you have questions about either form or need assistance with your submission, please contact us at **community-manager@ceph.org**.

We look forward to hearing from you and supporting the continued growth of the Ceph community!
