The Ceph User + Developer Monthly Meeting is a virtual platform that has long been in our community for encouraging discussion and collaboration between users and developers. The overarching goal of these meetings is to elicit feedback from the users, companies, and organizations who use Ceph in their production environments. To follow upcoming events, visit these links:
