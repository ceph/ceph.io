### Bringing Ceph back to Seattle

Join us for a full-day celebration of Ceph in Seattle, bringing together the people and ideas
driving open-source storage forward.

Connect with Ceph experts, core contributors, customers, and partners as they dive into the 
current state of the project, highlight recent improvements, preview what's on the roadmap, 
and share the latest community news. Stick around afterward for a networking reception to 
meet fellow community members.
